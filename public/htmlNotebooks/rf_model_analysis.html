Download PLP Results¶

Once your workflow successfully completed, you can download zip archive with initial workflow parameters, training execution logs, trained model PKL file, and training related plots.

To download your trained model, please follow these steps:

  • Visit CADC website https://qa-mickey.planx-pla.net/
  • If you are not logged in, click Login link on the top right conner and log in using your credentials
  • Click on the Apps icon on the top bar
  • Ensure your team selected properly in the Team Project field below the top bar. Change your team if needed by clicking Edit icon and selecting new team
  • Click on the Results App
  • In the list of workflows find your workflow and click on the corresponding Results button, then click Download All Results

This will start the downloading process.

Let's unpack zip archive with these simple commands.

Please change initial variables' values accordingly:

In [4]:
# Change path to your download folder path
download_path = '.'

# Change workflow id to workflow id you downloaded
plp_id = 'plp-f95hm'

# Change destination to your prefered destination:
destination_path = "."

Unpack downladed zip archive to the specified destination:

In [5]:
zip_path = os.path.expanduser(f'{download_path}/{plp_id}.zip')
destination_path = os.path.expanduser(destination_path)

with zipfile.ZipFile(zip_path) as zip_ref:
    zip_ref.extractall(destination_path)

plp_path = f'{destination_path}/{plp_id}'
